Company Profile


The Prodemy Book Store is an Indonesian Online Book Store Company with a mission to educate Indonesian through reading habits, especially to educate the nation's children as a milestone who will carry on the nation's struggle.

The Prodemy Book Store makes books prices cheaper, because we helps customers save on searching, communication and transportation costs. With PBS, Indonesians become more savvy in digital financial products, boosting financial literacy and inclusion in the country.

We always provide high quality previews for every book. We also perform visual inspections before our customer's order is shipped, because our costumers satisfaction is the main thing for us.

Feel free to contact us at any time if you require more information on any product in our store.
